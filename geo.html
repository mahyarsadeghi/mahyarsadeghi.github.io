Geo Analysis


Geo Distribution of the Best Selling Music Artists' Sales ($)

The first map shows the total amount of music sales earned by each country, thanks to their best selling music artists.
The increasing of the intensity of green scale color follows the increasing of the sales amount.



Geo Distribution of the Best Selling Music Artists' TCU (units)

The second map, instead, displays the total amount of music units (TCU) sold by best selling music artists of each country.
The increasing of the intensity of blue scale color follows the increasing of the TCU.



Geo Distribution of the Best Selling Music Artists

This last map want to show the number of best selling music artists for each country.
The increasing of the intensity of purple scale color follows the increasing of the number of music artists.
