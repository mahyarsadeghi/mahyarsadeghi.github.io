Time Analysis



Period of activity's distribution of best selling music artists through the years.

The first chart of this section is an histogram
that shows the distribution of the artists' period of activity among the years.
Hovering the bars of the charts it is possible to see what are the range of years to which each bar is related.




Period of activity's distribution of the top 10 genres through the years

This second chart compares the distribution of two genres's period of activity through the years, using two histograms.
With the specific dropdown menu is possible to select among different genres' comparison, considering the top 10 genres in terms of number of artists.
Clicking on the genres' names in the legend the associated part of the histogram is hidden and re-clicking is showed again.




Trends of the top 5 genres during the time

The third chart of time analysis is a multi-line plot that explain
how the best selling music artists' top 5 genres are evolved during the time.
Again clicking on the genres' names in the legend the associated line is hidden and re-clicking is showed again.




Total sale's amount of the top 5 genres in each of the top 5 countries 'till 2023

The last chart is a stacked bar chart that displays how much sales the top 5 genres
(considering the number of artists) have gained for each of the top 5 countries, until the 2023
Hovering each part of each bar we can see the exact amount of sales of that genre for the associated country.
